About the Event

The SITWARE students association of Computer Science & Engineering department conducts SITWARE Technical events every semester. As a part of this, we have planned to conduct technical events for this semester via both Online and Offline.

Why Participate?

  1. Opportunity.  This is a great opportunity to bring your technical talents out and get the recognition you deserve!
  2. Cash Prize for Winners. Winners of each event will get cash prize. 
  3. It's Free! No participation fee for any event. 

Events

1. Dates and Rules for each event is given below.
2. Participants should register individually for each event.
3. In case of protests, the decision of judges on the result of any event will be considered as final.

Quiz n' Connect

Date : Will be announced soon

This event will have two rounds. Participants should register themselves as a team of two members. When registering for this event, please make sure you enter your pair's name in the appropriate field. Your pair should also do a separate registration for this event and should mention your name as his/her pair.
Failing to do so will make both the contestants ineligible for the event.


Round 1 : Quiz
• The Objective of participants in this round is to give correct answers to all the given tech related multiple choice questions which gives points.
• The participants who have reached the minimum no of points required for the final round will proceed to the same
Round 2 : Connections
• In this round, participants will be shown a set of images for each question and they should figure out the term/word that these images collectively represent. Each correct answer will give certain number of points
• There will be a target score and the team to first reach the target score will be declared as the winners

Programming Contest

Date : Will be announced soon

This event will have one round. Participants should register themselves as individuals. The contest will be held separately for 2nd and 3rd years.
• The participants will be provided with Problem Statements/Code with bugs.  
• The Objective of participants is to write code/debug the given code such that the code's output matches the Expected output.
• Each Problem will have a certain number of points which is based on the difficulty of the problem. Points will be awarded for every solved problem. 
• The time and space complexity of the given solution may also be taken into consideration when awarding points.
• The participant with most number of points at the end of the contest will be declared as the winner.


Tech Research

Date : Will be announced soon 

This event will have one round. Participants should register themselves as individuals.
• Each participant will be provided with a topic and some time for the participant to do research about . Participants can choose any one of the problems.
• The Objective of participants is to present an explanation/a presentation on the given topic and answer the questions asked to them during the presentation.
• The winner will be selected on the basis of clarity of explanation, understanding on the topic and justification of answer.
